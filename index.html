Harvest Profit

Below are just a few of the projects I created for Harvest Profit. Most of my work was designing interfaces for their subscription-based web app for farmers.

Onboarding

This is the first thing a customer sees after they purchase access to the software. Previously, there was no onboarding — users were just dumped into the web app. I designed something that helps you get everything into the app so you can use the software right away.

Here is a demo of the onboarding process.

Crop Planner

This was one of the last big projects I worked on with Harvest Profit. Farming can get complicated fast, as I learned working on this project. On their software, a field can have one or more plantings, each planting one or more crops, and each planting can be subdivided between different farmers or businesses.

My job was to take what they had (left screenshot), add a few new features, apply all kinds of design best practices, and make the whole thing easier to use. Take a look at it here.
