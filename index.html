I am a designer. I delight in distilling complex ideas and have a tremendous passion for organization. I find great satisfaction in sorting and optimizing things — from the drawers in my kitchen to the interfaces I design. In the last seven years of my professional work I found my greatest passion is creating simple and beautiful ways to use things. I push hard for easy to understand interfaces, designs centered on users, and clear typography.
