The secret to making websites, and to computers in general:

Computers only do what they are told

"Well, duh!" I bet you're thinking. But it's worth remembering when you get stuck. Working with computers is the easiest complicated thing there is. Computers only do what you tell them to do. The rainbow effect, or the hopping emoji, and all the other stuff here is here because I told a computer to do it.
