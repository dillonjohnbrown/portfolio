Swanson Health Products

2011 — 2018

swansonvitamins.com

I worked at Swanson Health Products for more than six years. My first position was in their warehouse while I completed my bachelors degree.

My first profressional position with the company was as a graphic designer, where I created marketing materials like emails and promotional graphics.

Later, I was promoted to do front-end web development work. This is where, with the help of a fantastic manager, my love of user interface was incubated.

Subscriptions

One of the biggest projects I worked on was a ground-up replacement of our subscription service. We used a third-party service for customers to subscribe to our products. We dumped the outside company and built a replacement in-house. The in-house build was a sprawling beast of a task, touching nearly every corner of the website and involving every department in the building.

I was responsible for developing the user account part of the project. Our old third-party implementation is on top, mine is below.

Forms*

The forms on the site were not easy to use. I fixed them. Now customers can more easily interact with the website.

* This and the other projects marked with a red asterisk are projects I conceived, designed, developed, and presented to management.

Create Account

The old page (top) was a user experience disaster. Well, I say page, but there were actually three slightly different pages where a customer could sign in or create an account. I whittled that down to a healthy single page (bottom), and made it a little better with the help of my previous work standardizing forms, tabs, and radio buttons.

Header Concept*

As you've noticed, the site is largely devoid of color and more than a little heavy-handed. I wanted to bring a more professional look to the site, starting with the background and header of the site.

This concept was seen as too radical of a change and never made it beyond the drawing board.
