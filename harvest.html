Crop Planner

Farming can get complicated fast. With this software farmers track the details of their fields. Each field can have one or more plantings, each planting one or more crops, and each planting can be subdivided between different farmers or businesses (called "share agreements" in my designs, and "entity splits" on the current page).

My job was to take what they had (left/top image), add a few new features and make the whole thing easier to use. Here is my work on the feature and here is where the page is today.

The biggest change I made was to separate the share agreements to their own tab. In addition to cleaning up the interface, this allows you to save and apply them to different plantings (e.g., you and your neighbor regularly share the work on the fields where your properties meet).
