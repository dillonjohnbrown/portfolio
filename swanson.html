Swanson Health Products

I worked at Swanson Health Products for more than six years where I earned four promotions. During my time as a designer/developer I worked on more than 300 projects. Here are some of my favorites.

mobile search

I was responsible for evolving our mobile search results to something a little more sane (right).

subscriptions

One of the biggest projects I worked on was a ground-up replacement of our subscription service. We used a third-party service for product subscriptions. Long story short, we dumped them and built a replacement in-house. I was responsible for designing and developing the user account part of the project.

Our old third-party implementation is on top, mine is below.

forms*

I took a mess of interface elements and unified them under one design language. Part of this was our form elements. Now all the forms look similar while being easy to use on any device.
